# Recon AI

## Users

Sales professionals, sales managers, account executives. Quota-driven, data-literate, skeptical of marketing hype. Value tools that save time, reduce friction, and deliver measurable ROI.

## Product purpose

AI-powered prospect research and cold calling scripts. Users get instant company intel, decision maker research, personalized calling scripts, pipeline tracking, and real-time buyer signals — everything needed to close more deals.

## Surfaces

- **Landing page** — marketing, hero, features, social proof, CTA
- **Dashboard** — lead discovery, pipeline kanban, scripts, scoring
- **Email** — transactional, onboarding, feature announcements
- **Social** — LinkedIn, Instagram posts and ads

## Tone and voice

Confident, direct, no hype. Second-person ("you"). Name the user's world (prospects, calls, deals, pipelines), not ours. Specific numbers required. Short sentences, action verbs, sentence case. No hype vocabulary (revolutionary, game-changing, 10x, cutting-edge, supercharge, seamless).

## Anti-references

- Marketing clichés ("unlock the power of AI")
- Stock photography (diverse teams around laptops)
- Gradient text, glassmorphism, 3D effects
- Nested cards, generic icon grids
- Parallax, scroll-jacking, confetti
- Title Case or ALL CAPS headings

## Strategic principles

1. **Stop guessing. Start closing.** — Every feature accelerates the path from first touch to close
2. **Real data, real results** — Verifiable stats only. Show, don't tell
3. **Minimal, confident design** — Dark canvas, one accent color, smooth motion. Premium without overkill
4. **User-centric vocabulary** — Copy reflects sales professionals' world, not product architecture
5. **Accessibility first** — WCAG AA+ contrast, semantic HTML, respects prefers-reduced-motion

## Register

Product (app UI, dashboard tools — design SERVES the product functionality, not vice versa)

## Key features for MVP

- Lead Discovery: Search + list display with color-coded scoring
- Pipeline Kanban: 4-column drag-drop visualization
- Cold Call Scripts: Typewriter reveal with copy functionality
- Lead Scoring: Numeric stats with visual progress and tags
