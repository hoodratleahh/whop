2. **Generate Scripts** — Get personalized cold calling scripts tailored to each prospect

## Data & Privacy

### Where is my data stored?
Your data is stored securely in your browser's local storage. We also provide an export feature in Settings so you can download your data as a backup anytime.

### Can I export my leads and data?
Yes! In the **Settings** panel, you can:
- **Download Backup** — Export all your leads, scripts, and stats as JSON
- **Import Data** — Restore data from a previous backup

This ensures you always have control of your data.

### Is my data synced across devices?
Currently, data is stored locally on each device/browser. We recommend using the backup/export feature to sync data across devices manually. Cloud sync is on our roadmap.

### Is my data private?
Absolutely. Your leads, scripts, and personal data are stored only on your device. We don't sell or share your data with anyone.

## Troubleshooting

### I can't access Recon AI after purchase
1. Make sure you're logged into Whop
2. Try visiting https://testol.wtf directly
3. If issues persist, check your Whop account membership status

### My leads disappeared!
Leads are stored in your browser. If you cleared your browser cache/cookies, data may be lost. We recommend using the **Download Backup** feature in Settings regularly.

### The app is loading slowly
- Try clearing your browser cache
- Disable browser extensions temporarily
- Check your internet connection
- Try a different browser

### I forgot to save my data before clearing browser cache
Unfortunately, locally-stored data can't be recovered if your browser cache was cleared. This is why we strongly recommend using the **Download Backup** feature regularly in Settings.

### How do I get my API key working?
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Scroll to API Keys section
3. Paste your OpenRouter or Groq API key
4. Save and test

Need help? See our setup guides for each API provider.
